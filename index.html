So... Who are Yu?

I'm Christina Yu,

a front-end developer from Windsor, Ontario, now living in Toronto!

> What did you study?

I strive to discover beauty in the world, and studied fine arts and design at OCAD U.

> But didn't you say you are a web developer?

I certainly am! I decided to take on a different path of life by studying at Juno College's Immersive Web Dev Bootcamp, and I now code my visions into life!
I love creating things and meticulously perfecting them. I'm always in a search of ways to make my creations look good in every possible angle.

> Cool! So what else do you do?

When I'm not coding, you can find me drawing characters from my favourite shows, playing video games, and making icons and emoticons for professionals or creators as a side gig.
I also enjoy walking down the river in the sunset occasionally. Oh yes, I live by a river!
