I'm Christina Yu.

It's not you, it's Yu.
So yes, it's me.
(My highschool teachers used to just call me "Hey, Yu!!!" and they loved it.)

I'm a touring drummer turned web-developer based out of west-end Toronto. Challenging myself everyday by trying to master new tools and concepts in web-development, as well as trying to tackle some sick drum-fills and funk beats. Fueled by the drumming of Afro-beat master Tony Allen, and kiwis and coffee, I spend my free time delving deep into not only learning about web-development trends, but also learning how to create thoughtful web design. Lately, Swiss Modernism has been my design obsession. I'm stoked on creating beautiful and engaging user experiences, and hope we can do that together! Let's build something!
